QuizDown

A trivia app where users can test their knowledge on a variety of topics and have their score displayed on the leaderboard.

  • Frontend: Javascript
  • Backend: Rails
  • Frontend Repo | Backend Repo
  • • Integration with trivia questions API to generate quizzes for a range of topics
  • • Users can choose between timed and non-timed quizzes
  • • Timed quizzes provide instant visual feedback for answer selection
